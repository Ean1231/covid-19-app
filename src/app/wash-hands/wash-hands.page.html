HOW should you wash your hands?


The steps listed below are set out in our hand-washing video and also illustrated by the WHO here. Using soap and water:

 1. Wet hands with water and apply enough
soap to cover all surfaces of the hands Rub
hands palm to palm, with fingers interlaced. 2. Rub hands palm to palm, with fingers
interlaced. 3. Rub the right palm over the back of the left
hand with interlaced fingers - and vice versa 4. Rub hands palm to palm again, with fingers
interlaced 5. Rub the back of your fingers into the
opposing palm with fingers interlaced 6. Rub rotationally with the left hand clasped
in right palm and vice versa 7. To clean the tips of the fingers, group them
into a point and rub them into the opposite
palm in a rotational pattern 8. Rinse hands thoroughly with running water 9. Dry hands thoroughly with a single-use towel 10. If the tap is not elbow operated, use the
same towel to turn off the tap.
